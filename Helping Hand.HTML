About Us


We are a Non-governmental organization(NGO) named helping hands.We are a bunch of people who help those peoples who are facing some sort of problems in any sectors like public facilities,social problems,home problems,Harrassment,etc and in any region of India.In case of public facilities, we first try to send your problems to the particular administration and in order to that they solve their problems.Feel free to complaint about anything, your identity will not be compromised.


Our Objective


Some times people are not able to complaint about their day to day problems and adjust with the these things.These things not only affect our day to day life but also our mental health and leads to new problems.And here comeso our NGO which aims on this thing to reduce their adjustment with problems and to make them
rid of this mental issue and help them to overcome this issue as soon as posiible.We are available online 24x7,
